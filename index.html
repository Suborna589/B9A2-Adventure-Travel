Tourism is travel for pleasure, and the commercial activity of providing and supporting such travel Another potential new tourism industry is virtual tourism.Other groupings derived from the above grouping:

The terms tourism and travel are sometimes used interchangeably. In this context, travel has a similar definition to tourism but implies a more purposeful journey. The terms tourism and tourist are sometimes used pejoratively, to imply a shallow interest in the cultures or locations visited. By contrast, traveller is often used as a sign of distinction.
